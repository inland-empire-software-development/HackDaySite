What is a Hackday?

Hackday is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, and others, often including domain experts, collaborate intensively on software projects.

Who should attend?

All level of skills in every area of web development are very much welcomed to attend and participate in this event.

What do I bring?

This is a hands on and technological event. Bring your laptops with code editor of your choice with any tools that will help you code. We will be supplying wi-fi, desks and power outlets.

What is the goal of Hackday?

Get you great exposure to working in teams and under pressure. Challenging your critical thinking and problem solving skills. How to deal with project management and version control.

How do I form or join a team?

We do encourage participants to start forming teams before the event. Slack your piers and find out who is attending and is looking for team members. still don't have one by the day of the event, no problem. Try to be there at 2:45 and we will help you find a team. Teams should not be any larger than 5, exclusions apply.

Will there be prizes?

there will be several prizes thanks to our sponsors!

Who owns my project?

The team that works on the project is the owner of the project. We do highly encourage you to share the code with others in the community or making code open source but it is not required to participate in the event.

Is there a Hackday Code of Conduct?

You bet, see it here.

How much time do I have to complete?

the event will last 4 hours. We encourage to keep on working on it and improving it after the event as well. you imagination is the limit. be sure to share your work with other.
